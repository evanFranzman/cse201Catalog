Moments can be viewed through rose-colored glasses. In my case, memories can be viewed, remembered and re-invented on the canvas. Vibrant collections of color-stories pay homage to the past and present revealing my ideal. I recreate my color memory into present snapshots inspired by patterns and bright cohesive color palettes that remind me of my Virginia childhood. These present moments are from travels with overall positive outlooks, but with very specific inner conflict. By visually bringing together personal struggle with the picture perfect, it gives me balance in truth and nostalgia. I hope the viewer will only see the masked image I portray, in hopes to reflect or take them to their own personal flawless, bucolic and seamlessly gleeful memories.
